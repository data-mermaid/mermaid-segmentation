# Welcome to MermaidSeg

MermaidSeg is a codebase for training and evaluating image segmentation models on coral reef photos in support of [MERMAID](https://datamermaid.org), a global coral reef monitoring platform.

This Wiki is for anyone joining the project: graduate students, researchers, and external collaborators. It covers how to set up your environment, how we work together as a team, and the practices that keep our science reproducible and our codebase healthy.
